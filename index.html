Pembroke Democrats

mission statement

Goals of Pembroke Democrats:
-To develop an understanding of the concerns, needs and expectations of the Pembroke community so that issues important to our community may be addressed.

-To identify, recruit and support Democratic candidates to run for local, state and federal positions.

-To develop a local awareness of the Pembroke Democrats via participation in local events and maintenance of email, and the website.

-To fundraise, write informational materials, support group functioning and community activities, voter registration and turnout for local and regional democratic events.

-To stimulate community awareness of candidate positions.
